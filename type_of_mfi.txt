type_of_mfi :
a) rng_avg_size = running average filter and it's size
b) raw_mag = raw magentic field
c) smt_mag = smoothed magnetic field (median filter used)
d) rot_mag = rotated magnetic field
e) but_bnd = butterworth band filter
f) pcw_fit = piecewise fit magnetic field
g) fit_raw = fit from raw magnetic field data
h) fit_smt = fit from smoothed magnetic field data
